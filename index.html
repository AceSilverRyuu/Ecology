Ecology Club

Ecology club is a way for kids to get closer to nature rather than using and relying on technology all the time. How many hours a day do you spend on any of your technological items?

Generally we try to meet every other monday after school and during some flex periods. The group has an ongoing project to turn one of the school courtyards into an outdoor classroom. We pick up garbage, do volunteer work (Living Lands and Waters for example).
