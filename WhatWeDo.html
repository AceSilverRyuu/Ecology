Rules and Regulations

Before the REAL fun can begin, there are some rules and requirments members have to take part in. These rules and requirements are ONLY there to make sure members are safe and having the best time they could possibly have.

First, there is ABSOLUTLEY no technology on this trip. This is so members can experience the wilderness in all of its glory.

Second, members are not to join other groups to create a large group (nine or more at a time).

Third, any and all soap can not be used in the water even if it is biodegradable. Soap needs soil to be biodegradable. We are here to appreciate nature, not destroy it.

Please avoid- Moose, Bears, Wolfs, Otters, Lynx, and Bobcats if on the trip.
